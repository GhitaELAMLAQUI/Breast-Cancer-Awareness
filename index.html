BREAST CANCER SYMPTOMS

Breast cancer is a disease in which cells in the breast grow out of control. There are different kinds of breast cancer. The kind of breast cancer depends on which cells in the breast turn into cancer. Different people have different symptoms of breast cancer. Some people do not have any signs or symptoms at all. Some warning signs of breast cancer are shown below.

Nipple turning inward

Nipple discharge

Lump in the underarm area

Swelling of part of the breast

Swelling of all breast

Skin dimpling

Breast or nipple pain

Skin irritation

    WHAT TO DO IF YOU SHOW SYMPTOMS OF BREAST CANCER ?
  • Do not panic, If breast cancer is found early, there are more treatment options and a better chance for survival.
  • Be sure to see your doctor right away.
